## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue, customer lifetime value (CLTV), and profitability.

This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI**, **Python** and **DAX** to analyze customer behavior, identify churn drivers, measure financial impact, and recommend business strategies for customer retention.

The solution follows a business storytelling approach, guiding stakeholders from high-level executive KPIs to detailed customer analytics, churn diagnostics, and financial performance.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are to:

- Understand customer demographics and segmentation
- Identify the major drivers of customer churn
- Measure Revenue at Risk due to churn
- Analyze Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV)
- Evaluate customer behavior across products and services
- Recommend actionable retention strategies
- Build an executive-ready interactive Power BI dashboard

# 📊 Key Business Insights

- Customer churn rate is approximately **34%**.
- Month-to-Month contracts experience the highest churn.
- Fiber Optic customers generate the highest revenue but also exhibit elevated churn.
- High Value customers contribute significantly to overall revenue.
- Competitor offerings and pricing are the leading churn drivers.
- Revenue at Risk exceeds **$2.6M**, highlighting the importance of targeted retention initiatives.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Prioritize retention campaigns for High Value customers.
- Encourage migration from Month-to-Month to annual contracts.
- Strengthen customer onboarding during the first 12 months.
- Review pricing strategies for Fiber Optic services.
- Develop loyalty programs for long-tenure customers.
- Focus retention efforts on customers with high Churn Scores.
